Normalization

Using wateRmelon, quantile normalize the experiments via betaqn method. There're 15 different normalization methods. Let's see what this paper has to say…

\( = M/(M + U + 100) \) where \( M \) indicates intensity of methylated and \( U \) not methylated. They conclude that quantile normalization yeilds markedly improved methods, using the 3 metrics they've derived.

Check out this paper for more info on \( \beta \) and \( M \) values.
